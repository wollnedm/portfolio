About Me

Hello! My name is Dani Wollner. I am a soon-to-be graduate from UW-Eau Claire. Welcome to my portfolio! I'll just recap by favorite things, passions, goals, and show off some cool photos I've taken and some writing samples. Read more to learn more about me!

Passions

I have a few main passions and fixations that are fairly synonomous with who I am! If you ask my friends to describe me, they will most likely say some or all of these.

  • Traveling: I love to visit new cities. I studied abroad in fall of 2024 and loved exploring new cities and countries. Ireland and China are first up next on my to-visit list!
  • Chai and Coffee: I just love a good chai latter or coffee. I really enjoy going to new coffee shops as much as possible and trying new drinks. I have a pretty expansive chai tier list for Eau Claire!
  • Hiking: Who does love a good hike? I'm an avid All Trails user and try to get a hike in wherever and whenever I can. My favorites in the area are Beaver Creek Reserve and Hoffman Hills.
  • Yello: I do have a pretty intense fixation on yellow. It's been my favorite color since 9th grade and I've only grown my yellow collection since then. I'll immediately like anything as long as it's yellow.
  • Books: Reading is the longest passion and love of my life. My whole family reads so it's been ingrained in me since I could...well, read!
  • Language: Languages are so interesting! Learning languages is so fun and fulfilling. I know a bit of French, Chinese, and Czech and would love to learn more.
  • Skyrim: It's a video game but I've been thoroughly obsessed with it since 6th grade. I couldn't exactly explain why, but I love the game to death and could talk about it for hours. I have to ban myself from playing Skyrim during the semester or I'll get too addicted to it!

Goals and Bucket List

My biggest goal in life is to work in book publishing! As I mentioned above, I love books and reading, and pretty much everything to do with books and reading. I've wanted to work with books my whole life, publishing would just fulfill all my dreams.
My other major life goals are achieving fluency in at least one other language, adopting a senior cat, and living abroad.
